Assessment Objectives

Explanation
KN definition formulas terminology techniques

Command words

Command word Explanation
Advise write down a suggested course of action in a given situation
Analyse examine in detail to show meaning, identify elements and the relationship between the
Assess make an informed judgement
Calculate work out from given facts, figures or informatio
Define give precise meaning
Evaluate judge or calculate the quality, importance, amount, or value of something
Explain set out purposes or reasons/make the relationships between things evident/provide why and/or how and support with relevant evidence
Identify name/select/recognise
Justify support a case with evidence/argumen
